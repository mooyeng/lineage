Establishment

Although Shan Mokuwani Dash Sobba rebelled against the eight kings of Limbuwan and made an agreement, it seems that the rule of Dashgadhi in Limbuwan must have been after 1300 AD. Before this, the Thebes had settled in Hangyakpung (Hastapuyak) around 1200 AD. Considering the time of Khokyahang Manyangwu and the time of Tingdihang Thebe, Tingdihang is about Around 1200 AD. Dorumba had come and settled. After a treaty with Dash Sobba during the reign of the 12th-generation Yoghavahang Thebe, Shan Mokuwani Tindolung Khokyahang began to rule in the Shenhang Yak (Hastapuyak) part. During the time Angbung Dorumba came and settled, the word Thebe was being established by leaving the Thebe Sen Yakthung. Looking at the structure of the caste of the Limbus, it is found that most of the tribes are formed on the basis of the identity of the place where the person concerned lives, the forest specific and the personal habits and physical constitution of the person. For example: Angbhang Thar from Aang Pangve, Yangyang made by shaking cock, Fenduwa made from iron worker, Thegim to those living in Thegu (hill), Nalbo to those living in Nalbo and Thallang to those living in Thalang etc. etc .....

Thebe's surname could have been established for any of the following reasons:

  • Around 1641 AD, Tamor Yangwarok of northern Limbuwan conquered the northern Panchthar, Ilam, Fakfok and Darjeeling thums and annexed them to the Sukhim kingdom. In the Sherpa Bhote language, Theva is the eldest or eldest. The Bhotes who lived in the neighboring village called the son of Senyakthung's eldest Judal as Theba Theba.

  • Thebe in Yakthung language means 'what is it?' This means that the person who is always accustomed to be called Thebe Thebe, may be called Thebe Thebe, and his descendants may later be called Thebe.

  • In the Yakthung language, "thu:pet" means a sitting posture that sits flat, but the foot touches the ground, and bends the knee with both hands. A person who is always in the habit of sitting only in this position may be called Thupa:et -> Thejepet -> The:pet -> Nepe -> Thebe.

  • It is said that the pickle made after eating mesu in kharwa for a few days is called "thebe". While jokingly telling a person who eats only such pickles to eat only Theve, his name may have become Thebe and later his children may have been called Thebe.
